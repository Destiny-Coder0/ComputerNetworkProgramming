About the Project

This website is designed as an online plant nursery system. It allows users to explore various types of plants, view categorized plant data, and submit an order form. The project includes five pages: a homepage, a detailed plant list, an order form, a table of plant features, and a page for additional highlights.

The system is designed for users who are interested in houseplants, garden care, or decorative plants. The goal is to provide a clean and user-friendly interface for plant lovers.
